About

With years of experience performing as a vocalist on luxury cruise ships, I have had the privilege of bringing music to audiences around the world. Classically trained in voice, my background spans a wide range of styles, from opera and musical theater to contemporary and jazz. My time at sea allowed me to refine my craft, adapt to diverse audiences, and deliver powerful performances in breathtaking settings.

Before my work on cruises, I was deeply involved in theater productions, where I developed a strong stage presence and a passion for storytelling through music. Now based in Ireland, I am available to perform at weddings, corporate events, private gatherings, and special occasions. Whether creating an elegant atmosphere with classical pieces or setting the tone with modern favorites, I tailor each performance to suit the event.
